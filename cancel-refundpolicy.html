Cancellation and Refund Policy

Cancellation:

Customers may cancel their services with Subhujo Technologies by submitting a cancellation request via email, phone, or in person. The cancellation request must include the customer's account information and reason for cancellation. Upon receipt of the cancellation request, Subhujo Technologies will process the cancellation and confirm it with the customer.

Refund:

Subhujo Technologies offers refunds on cancelled services. Refunds will be processed in the original form of payment. If the original form of payment was made via credit card, the refund will be issued to the same credit card. If the original form of payment was made via debit card or cash, the refund can only be processed in person.

For any questions or concerns about our cancellation and refund policy, customers can contact Subhujo Technologies at info@subhujo.com.
